Mac and Cheese

Decadent and cheesy mac and cheese, perfect for the holidays as side or the main course.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of uncooked pasta shells
  • 1/4 cup of salted butter
  • 3 tbsp of all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1 cup of cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup of parmesan cheese
  • 2 tsp of smoked paprika

Steps

  1. Melt the butter on a medium saucepan over medium heat
  2. Slowly whisk in the flour into the melted butter
  3. Pour in the milk, a quarter cup at a time, in to pan
  4. Mix in the cheeses into the sauce and reduce on low heat
  5. Cook the pasta according to instructions on the packaging
  6. Once the pasta is cooked and strained, mixed into the cheese sauce
  7. Season with salt, pepper, and paprika to taste
