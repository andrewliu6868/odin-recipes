Fried Rice

Delicious and quick fried rice recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup of diced carrots
  • 1/2 cup of frozen peas
  • 1/4 cup of canned corn
  • 1/4 cup of chopped celery
  • 2 tbsp of vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp of soy sauce
  • 2 tsp of sesame oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups of refrigerated rice

Steps

  1. Season a wok with oil on high heat
  2. Pour in the eggs and season to taste
  3. Once the eggs have set and are halfways cooked, set it aside/li>
  4. Stir in the vegetables for 30 seconds
  5. Throw in the rice and mix in the soy sauce and sesame oil to taste
  6. Toss the eggs back in and cook for another minute
