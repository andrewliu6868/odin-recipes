Omelette

Healthy omelette to start your day off right!

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp of salted butter
  • 1/2 cup of sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 slice of black forest ham
  • 1 chopped parsley

Steps

  1. Melt the salted butter over medium heat
  2. Reduce the heat to low and whisk in the eggs
  3. Once the egg sets (edges start to solidify), repeat the following steps
    1. Carefully pull in the set edge to the center of the omelette
    2. Tilt the pan so that the remaining raw egg replaces the edge that was pulled
    3. Repeat the process until no raw egg remains
  4. Add in the cheese and ham, then fold the omelette in half
  5. Cook on both sides for about 20 seconds then season with salt and peper to taste
  6. Garnish with parsley and serve
